AIM Of MiniBANK

The main aim of designing and developing the MiniBANK DBMS/php based engineering project is to provide efficient net banking facilities,using this net banking facilities user can open an account, transfer fund, deposit fund, withdrawal, and search all his transactions.

What We Offer

Here are some of the features we tried to offer in our MiniBANK banking project.

View Balance

Firstly login your account using username and password and your balance would be displayed with other info such as last credited amount as well as debited amount.

Transfer Funds

When you select transfer funds,you'll be asked where to transfer the money to and from and the amount.

Transactions

This module display the the transactions made by the customer in the particular date with transaction details.

Administrative Control

This module contains the administrative functions such as view all virtual account, transactions, approve and delete virtual accounts.

Login/Register

Virtual account holders can login/register into the system using this module. Thus this is the login page for customers in the website

Deposit/Withdrawal

This is the module to deposit/withdrawal money from virtual bank account holders from their specified account number.
